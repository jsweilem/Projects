GroupMe Analyzer

All of Your Messaging Statistics

Welcome to the GroupMe Analyzer!


GroupMe provides the ability to export all of the messaging data of any group you have been in!
While this data is neatly packaged for you, it may not be evident how to actually learn more
about your data. That is why we built this tool! After extracting GroupMe's data, you can input
it here and we will take care of the breakdown for you!

It is highly recommended that you take a look at our "How To" page to understand how to use the tool!
